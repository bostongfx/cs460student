RO.ME "3 DREAMS OF BLACK"

RO.ME is a very creative interactive demo and it incorporates several mediums to create a full project. It was released shortly after WebGL's official release in 2011, and served as one of the first large scale showcases of WebGLThe second scene takes place on a train in a plains and the user's gaze causes black ruptures to emerge amoung the green background. The final area is a desert and the user here is briefly able to create short shrubbery until eventually taking off as a flight of birds, allowing them to fly across the terrain and visit the previous zones. In particular, I was impressed by the desert section, and controlling the birds to fly between the different scenes. The transitions were also smooth and the demo ran very well. The impact of the demo showed that browser based hardware accelerated graphics were possible, opening a whole new avenue for web applications.
