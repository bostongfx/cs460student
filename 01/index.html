RO.ME "3 DREAMS OF BLACK"

RO.ME is a very cool webgl demo! I can see a lot of animals ruining through different environments, and the environments change as the music goes on. It starts from a some sort of realistic but non interactive black and white scene (real time rendering), and then it slowly transitions to a low poly colorful scene that I can do some interactions such as moving the camera and even adding objects to the movie. This demo came out right after the release of webgl. It shows the potential of webgl and this kind of framework. We can do a lot of cool and artistic things with webgl, and give audience a immersive 3D experience that could just run natively in the browser.

Technology: 2D graphics, videos, 3D graphics are all merged in this demo. The demo is actually a real time rendering model. The only usage of HTML and JavaScript create a 3D interactive environment. WebGL is hardware-accelerated 3D graphics without the use of plug-ins. It use your video card to do real time rendering, so it is very fast and smooth. It enables you to use your mouse to move your angle of vision, to move around objects and put objects in the scene.

Comparison: Unlike the RO.ME experience that I can feel the passion and the energy from different animals and different environments from the video, the Le Voyage Azarien is a relaxing and quiet forest experience that gives me peace and calm after watching it. It creates a vivid and peaceful forest environment with some birds singing and some leaves rustling that makes feel like I am actually in a forest. It uses LIDAR sensor to scan a real world tree, which is different from how the RO.ME build their environment. The interactions are boarder in RO.ME because I can put some stuffs I like in the video to make it unique, however, in Le Voyage Azarien, the interactions are minimal. I am only change some camera angles or zoom in and out. Last but not least, two of the videos have great music, but the RO.ME is music driven storytelling where the music is the soul and spine of the story.
