RO.ME "3 DREAMS OF BLACK"

RO.ME is an incredible piece of technology utilizing the capabilities of WebGL. I have never really seen/experienced a piece of interactive technology in this manner in 2025, and this project was created over 14 years ago, where it was probably considered revolutionary at the time. I've played plenty of video games, but to have a constantly moving movie/video play while you can interact with it and change things really astounded me. You have parts of the video where the camera pans to wherever your mouse is pointing/moving towards, other parts where you paint the ground actively black with your mouse as you travel through it, animals running around you which transforms into other animals actively while you're still trying to play with it and figure out how the interactivity works. Truly a great piece of artistic design.

WebGL, HTML5, Three.js, WebGL Inspector
