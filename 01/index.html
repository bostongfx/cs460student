RO.ME "3 DREAMS OF BLACK"

RO.ME: 3 Dreams of Black (2011) is a pioneering interactive music film built with early WebGL and Three.js. It blends real-time 3D, animation, and music into a browser-based story you can navigate and interact with—proving the web could deliver cinematic 3D without plugins. Its release inspired a wave of modern web graphics and interactive storytelling on the open web.
