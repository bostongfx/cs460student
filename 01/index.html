RO.ME "3 DREAMS OF BLACK"

RO.ME is a very cool webgl demo!
It is really interesting that you can using your mouse to control the camera in the music video, and even interact with animals likes birds. It is basically a simple video game that you can play right in your browser, without downloading anything.
And the fact that it was created 14 years ago is just crazy. It is still amazing even today.

Technology: WebGL, THREE.js, html5 and canvas, javascript, Chrome Experiments
