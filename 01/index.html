RO.ME "3 DREAMS OF BLACK"

RO.ME is an interactive movie made with WebGL. It uses both 2D videos and 3D landscapes which run in the browser with WebGL, sometimes mixing the two media to create a "2.5D" experience at times. It was released in 2011, which is the same year that WebGL was first released. The creation of RO.ME pushed the boundaries of WebGL and pioneered further development for the technology.

Technology:

  • HTML5
  • WebGL
  • Three.js
  • Blender

Comparison: Mark Clennon Photo Diary
The RO.ME movie focuses on the narrative, where the spectator is on a tour through the various "dreams" in the movie. Mark Clennon's personal website constructs a globe full of photos from his everyday, where the end user in the middle of the globe, almost in the shoes of Mark himself.
