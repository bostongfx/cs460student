RO.ME "3 DREAMS OF BLACK"

RO.ME is a mix of video, music, and 2D + 3D graphics to show off what is possible using WebGL. As you go along in the story you can move your mouse to interact with the environment. In some parts your mouse will make a blur effect and in others it will lead animals and leave behind a trail of a different enviroment. This demo was meaningful because it showed what can be done when you hardware acceleration, and shows whats possible with just writing HTML and JavaScript code. Made in 2011, it showed that you can enter an inteactable 3D world just from your browser.

RO.ME is coded with HTML and JavaScript and uses WebGL with hardware acceleration to render in real time inside your browser.

Emotion In this experience, you have full manual control over the speed of your progression unlike in RO.ME where your progress is based on the different parts of the song. This expereince is used as a way to for the company to show off what kind of UI and visuals they can create. Both Emotion and RO.ME show what can be done with web graphics, but Emotion is promoting their service rather than soley demonstrating whats possible.

"RO.ME: The 3D WebGL Experience (2011) was an interactive story by Google Creative Lab and Unit9 that showcased WebGL as a powerful new browser technology; it was significant because it was the first large-scale demo without plugins, blended art, music, and storytelling, and proved the web could deliver immersive 3D experiences that inspired future creative projects" (ChatGPT)
