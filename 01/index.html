RO.ME "3 DREAMS OF BLACK"

"3 Dreams of Black" doubles as a prominent demo of WebGL technology and an experimental film based on a concept album. Over music by two musicians, Danger Mouse and Daniele Luppi, the film goes through scenes of interactive visuals right in your browser.

Technology: WebGL, implemented with the Three.js library

Comparison: "no-fishing.net" is a campaign created by the organization "Sea Shephard" designed to protect fish populations by cleaning up the ocean and combatting over-fishing. Their website makes use of WebGL to give visitors an engaging summary of the work they do and how you can help. Like RO.ME, no-fishing.net starts with a short traditional video section. But after the video, the site takes a different approach from RO.ME by leaving the user a model of the Earth which they can rotate and interact with to read more at their own pace.
