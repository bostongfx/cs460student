RO.ME "3 DREAMS OF BLACK"

RO.ME is a very cool webgl demo! RO.ME is an interesting example of displaying webGL as an interactive project. It combines storytelling with animation in a way that it completely unique to other websites I've seen myself. It was created in 2011 and was one of the first large-scale WebGL projects that existed at the time. It showed that just your browser could handle large-scale projects such as this.
