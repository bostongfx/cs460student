RO.ME "3 DREAMS OF BLACK"

The RO.ME demo is an interactive film made for the music album "Rome" that uses WebGL and allows hardware-accelerated 3D graphics without an extension. It was created in 2011 and WebGL was still very new back then. Its complexity for a brand new API allowed educational institutions to enhance the learning experience and to expand its accessibility. On top of that, it encouraged web developers to experiment more.

Technology: Three.js and Processing.js from JavaScript, the tool WebGL Inspector and HTML5.
