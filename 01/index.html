RO.ME "3 DREAMS OF BLACK"

RO.ME is a very cool webgl demo! It incorpates video, 2d animation and 3d visual graphics with the song/dreams to create an immersive and interactive world/narrative. It had very smooth transitions between the different forms of media. In 2d you could start moving the camera slightly, while in 3d there was free control including having flora and animals being rendered where the mouse pointed at. The visual designs encapsulted the mood and story of the song very well such as having the scene where the mouse turned everything black. Considering this video was created around 14 years ago it had showcased the possibilities of visual graphics in web browsers especially for music/songs.

Technology: RO.ME used HTML, JavaScript, WebGL, audio, 3D visuals created with code, uses video card to render graphics, three.js.

Comparison: https://le-voyage-azarien.art/ Compared to the RO.ME, the concept of this webgl is simpler. A person narrates about the forest and trees while we are given blob-like visualaztions of the tree. The visualization and sound makes a deep and profound atmosphere for the narration of the story. It shows how the person is giving appreciation for the tree and how it relates to themselves. Unlike the RO.ME it gives a more direct story telling with simpler visuals. There wasn't free camera movement compared to the RO.ME and no visuals being created with the mouse. I think it focuses a lot more on the person's words with the visuals as the support while the RO.ME sometimes feels like the song is supporting the visuals more.
