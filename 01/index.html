RO.ME "3 DREAMS OF BLACK"

RO.ME is an interactive WebGL music video with first person storytelling about three dreams.
Dream 1: We follow the main character driving alone through a end of the world land, past ruined buildings. She reaches a house like room, spends the night, and eats simple cooked beans. Then she fall asleep, close her eyes.
Dream 2: The main character then opens her eyes, incident she wokes up. The visual style shifts. The character then drives beyond the zone into a wider world. As you move the mouse, grasses sprout and animals run across the mouse you pointed at.
Dream 3: The visual style shifts again. The story start from the main character wake up on a train. There are oxs run along the train. The character gets off the train and wanders through a open land, continuing the journey on foot. While you move the mouse, the world continues to shift. More subtle stuffs reacted, like dust and small birds.

Technology: WebGL, 2D Graphic, Video, 3D Graphic, Real time Rendering, Google Chrome, Javascript, HTML
