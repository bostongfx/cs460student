RO.ME "3 DREAMS OF BLACK"

3 Dreams of Black is an interactive WebGL music experience created by Danger Mouse, Daniele Luppi and Chris Milk. It's an experience that features a combination of 2D graphics, video and 3D graphics all playing together through WebGL During the time of its release, it was one of the first extensive demonstrations of real-time 3D graphics that was able to run natively in the browser without additional plugins. It essentially showcased the capabilities of WebGL to transform the browser into an open source video game console. This project served as inspiration for a future generation of web based graphics and showcased how WebGL could be used for creative storytelling.

Technology: WebGL, 3D
