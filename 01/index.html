RO.ME starts with a short film of someone falling asleep. Then it feels like a dream, where we travel through the dream and visit certain stops. At one point it is a geometric building/ruinscape. Another aspect we are taken on a train, where we see buffalos running and they turn into demon like figures. Technology: The technology used for RO.ME is webgl. That is used to create the 3d scape as you travel through the different dreams. It also has a combination of different sounds and music as well as actual video that starts off the project before the person falls asleep. It uses 3d renders to render all the animals we see, the brids, deer, fish, and other animals we see.

It uses the same technology except it is a different experience, givign emphasis on the stone used to create the objects and the house being built in a quarry with having an about section and gallery which acts more like a art gallery than a music video/walkthrough since you can go at your own pace.
