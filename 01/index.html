RO.ME "3 DREAMS OF BLACK"

RO.ME is an immersive WebGL music video that allows the visitor of the website to move their view according to where their cursor is located. The website incorporates elements of not only recorded videos/images, but also 2D art and 3D art in order to make the whole experience stimulating in a very creative way. The film takes the visitor through several different scenes and also allow the visitor to control where the animals move. This web film was made in 2011, the same year that WebGL 1.0 was officially released. This project demonstrated how powerful browsers could really be and what awesome works could be produced!

Technology: Three.js, WebGL, HTML5, JavaScript

Comparison: House of Dreamers
The House of Dreamers website was made WebGL, Transition, and GSAP. It features a fluid effect on mouse cursor movement, smooth page transitions from one 3D dreamlike scene to another, and looping music. The graphics are beautifully rendered; it's what you would expect from a game. There is also slight camera movement as the the cursor moves and to butterflies periodically fluttering in the foreground of the scenes.
